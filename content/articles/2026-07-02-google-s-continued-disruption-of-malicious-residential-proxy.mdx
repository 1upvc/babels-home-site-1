Google took coordinated action against the NetNut residential proxy network by disabling accounts and sharing intelligence on its SDKs and C2 infrastructure. Because many other brands resell this whitelabeled botnet, a single disruption is insufficient; operators simply purchase capacity from competitors when their own networks are degraded.
