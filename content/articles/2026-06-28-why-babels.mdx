Detection engineering is the practice of building, testing, and maintaining the
logic behind security alerts. Done well, it is the difference between signal and
noise. With the rise of AI and GenAI, teams need sharper capabilities and better
insight to keep pace.

**Babels** is our answer. We build open-source tooling and share curated insight
so cyber defense practitioners can raise the quality of their detections. Our work
spans three areas:

- **Tooling** — open-source detection engineering solutions on Elastic and SIGMA.
- **AI Integration** — LLM-powered workflows (including an MCP server) to convert,
  validate, and deploy rules conversationally.
- **Insight** — this Articles feed: curated news and original thought pieces on
  detection engineering, delivered at a frequency of your choosing.
